Pre-Encryption and Post-Decryption Hooks
Capturing and reading encrypted outbound network packets in transit is somewhat difficult, especially when there are asymmetric keys at work. Instead though… we could hook into the encryption routine, and dump the message somewhere to read later. When it comes to decryption, we can just wait for the decryption to finish, and then dump the decrypted message somewhere as well!
